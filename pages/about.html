About AI Safety Foundations

Learn about our mission, values, and the team behind AI Safety Foundations.

Mission Statement

AI Safety Foundations empowers the next generation with knowledge and tools to understand, advocate for, and contribute to the safe and ethical development of artificial intelligence through accessible, engaging education.

Core Values

Accessibility

We make complex AI safety concepts understandable and relevant to young audiences without sacrificing accuracy or depth. Our content meets learners where they are, using language, examples, and formats that resonate with high school and undergraduate students.

Scientific Integrity

We maintain rigorous standards for accuracy and nuance in all educational content. We distinguish between established knowledge, emerging research, and open questions in the field, avoiding both alarmism and complacency.

Engagement

We create learning experiences that are inherently interesting and interactive. We use storytelling, visualization, gamification, and real-world examples to make AI safety concepts memorable and meaningful.

Inclusivity

We design our educational approach to welcome diverse perspectives and backgrounds. We recognize that AI safety requires input from many disciplines and viewpoints, and we actively work to make our content accessible to students from all backgrounds.

Practical Application

We emphasize actionable knowledge and skills that empower young people to engage with AI safety in tangible ways. We provide clear pathways for students to apply their learning through projects, career exploration, and community participation.

Community

We foster a supportive community where young people can explore AI safety concepts together. We create spaces for discussion, collaboration, and mentorship that extend learning beyond content consumption.
